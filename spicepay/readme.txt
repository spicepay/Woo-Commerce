SpicePay allows you to accept bitcoin payments on your WooCommerce store.
 
== Description ==
 
The more online payment options your customers have, the better and revolutionary blockchain technology empowers you to connect with customers in a new way, with a world of benefits. SpicePay's blockchain payment platform provides an easy, low-cost way to accept online payments in bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies, and transact with anyone, anywhere. 

Our Wordpress plugin, compatible with all Woocommerce stores was uniquely designed to meet the needs of online businesses. You can now easily install our plugin to begin accepting cryptocurrency payments right away. Set up is easy and our support staff is reachable 24/7 to help out with any questions or concerns you might have!
 
Key features
* Price in your local currency, let customers pay with bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies
* Receive instant payment confirmation (e.g. for virtual goods)
* Have an overview of all your cryptocurrency payments in your [SpicePay merchant dashboard](https://www.spicepay.com/dashboard.php)
* Refund your customers in bitcoin in your [SpicePay merchant dashboard](https://www.spicepay.com/dashboard.php)
 
== Installation ==
 
Get started by signing up for a [SpicePay merchant account.](https://www.spicepay.com/register.php)

You must also have already installed the Woocommerce plugin from the WordPress Plugin Directory. 

Install the latest version of the SpicePay plugin for Woocommerce.
1. Navigate to your WordPress Admin Panel and select Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin.
2. Select the downloaded plugin and click "Install Now". Select "Activate" to complete installation. 

**Connecting SpicePay and Woocommerce**

Go to [https://www.spicepay.com/tools.php](https://www.spicepay.com/tools.php)
Click on "add site" button
Fill the form. 
For callback Secret field set a random secret string
For callback URL use: http://your-site.com/?wc-api=WC_Spicepay (replace your-site.com with your site)
Click "Save changes"

Log in to your WordPress admin panel and select "WooCommerce" > "Settings" > "Checkout" > "SpicePay" to access the configuration settings for the plugin.
You can also access the configuration settings by navigating to "Plugins" and clicking the "Settings" link for this plugin.
Update settings with site ID

Congratulation! Your customers will now be able to check out with bitcoin on your site.
